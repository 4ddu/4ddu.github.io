Propel Initiative

Empowering underprivileged students through science, innovation, and real-world problem solving.

Our Mission

Propel Initiative conducts Shark Tank–style science fairs in underprivileged schools, helping students identify real problems, build innovative solutions, and connect top projects with potential sponsors.

What We Do

Problem Identification

Students learn how to observe their surroundings and identify meaningful problems worth solving.

Research & Innovation

We guide students to research, ideate, and develop practical science-based solutions.

Pitch & Prototype

Students create presentations or prototypes and pitch them to specialists and sponsors.

Sponsorship Pathway

Top projects from each school are connected with sponsors for funding and mentorship.

Program Structure (1 Week)

  • Day 1: Orientation & Group Formation
  • Day 2: Presentation, Prototype & Financial Planning
  • Days 3–4: Student Preparation
  • Day 5: Mock Session & Feedback
  • Day 6: Final Pitch, Results & Recordings

Total Time: ~ timings are flexible, though ususally it is close to 7 hours across 6 working days

Roles & Responsibilities

Core Team

Planning, coordination, content delivery, and sponsor outreach.

Teacher Coordinator

Acts as a bridge between students and the Propel team via WhatsApp.

Specialists

Provide expert guidance, validate ideas, and offer feedback.

Media Team

Handles documentation, video recordings, and social media outreach.
